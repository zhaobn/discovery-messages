Instructions

In this game, you are a space explorer. You just landed on a faraway planet. The surface of this planet is dotted with valuable crystals that can be harvested for special energy points.

Some of these crystals can also be fused to create new and much more powerful crystals. However, scientists still do not fully understand why some pairs of crystals can fuse while others cannot.

Your goal is to harvest as many energy points as possible by collecting, combining, and harvesting crystals. Explore the planet, experiment with fusions, and maximise your score.

Use arrow keys to move your rover across the planet. Everything else is done with Spacebar or D.

↑ ↓ ← → Move your rover
Spacebar Pick up the crystal you're standing on (rover must be empty)
Spacebar Attempt a fusion — while holding a crystal and standing on another
Spacebar Harvest for energy points — while holding a crystal and standing on an empty tile
D Drop the crystal you're holding

Your rover holds one crystal at a time. You must pick up, fuse, or harvest before you can interact with another crystal.

Harvesting is the only way to earn energy points. Fusing alone doesn't score—you need to harvest the resulting crystal.

A running log will track every fusion you've attempted and every crystal you've harvested, so you always know how your actions have been spent.

Naturally-occurring crystals on this planet are red. Each red crystal is worth 1 energy point and can regenerate once after being harvested.

A crystal's colour tells you exactly how many points it's worth:

  • 🟥  1 point
  • 🟧  10 points
  • 🟨  100 points
  • 🟩  1,000 points
  • 🟦  10,000 points
  • 🟪  100,000 points

When you discover a successful fusion, you'll produce a new crystal worth 10× as many energy points as the originals. For example, fusing two red crystals (1 pt each) gives you one orange crystal (10 pts).

⚡ Your rover has fuel for up to 40 actions. Every fusion attempt and every harvest uses one action. Use them wisely!

Other people have already played this game. Before finishing, they were asked to leave a message for the next player — that's you.

They were told their bonus payment would depend on how well the next player performs, so they had a genuine incentive to be helpful.

On the next screen, you'll be able to read their messages and save notes to a notebook you can refer back to during the game.

🔄 Pay it forward: At the end of the game, you'll have the chance to leave a message for a future participant playing the same game on the same planet. Share any knowledge or tips you think will help them harvest more energy points. Your bonus will depend both on how well you play and how well they perform.

When you're ready, click Read messages to see what previous players left for you — then begin. Good luck!
